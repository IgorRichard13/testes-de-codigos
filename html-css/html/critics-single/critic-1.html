"Are they your friends?"

      In our daily lives we must be cautious with who we call a friend, since there is a difference between FRIEND and COLLEAGUE. According to the online dictionary DICIO, the definition of friend is:


"A person you love, you can trust, with whom you have a relationship of affection, companionship, friendship."


      People often confuse a friend with a colleague, since a friend is someone who has an intimate and trusting relationship with you, whereas a colleague is the one who is in the same socializing as you and normally will not have any intimate bond. That said, you have to be careful with who you call a friend, because many people will harm you, want you bad or even not care about yours. situation, but a true friend is one who celebrates by your side and cries with you. Be careful, because it is in the most extremes that you find who your true friends are.
